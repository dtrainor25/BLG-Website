About Bristal Lane Group

We built the firm we wished existed — so independent advisors could have the infrastructure of a large institution without sacrificing their freedom.

Our Mission

Passionate about your success.

Bristal Lane Group was founded by entrepreneurs who understood firsthand the challenges of building an independent financial services firm. We knew that advisors who chose independence deserved more than just freedom — they deserved a real partner.

Our mission is simple: provide the comprehensive back-office infrastructure that allows you to focus entirely on your clients and your growth. We become the operational backbone of your firm so you never have to choose between great client service and sound business management.

We are passionate about your success™ — and that means showing up with expertise, commitment, and a genuine stake in your outcomes.
