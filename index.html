What We Offer

A Full Suite of Back-Office Solutions

From startup to succession, we support every stage of your firm's lifecycle with dedicated expertise.

Tax & Accounting

Outsourced CFO services, bookkeeping, tax planning, financial statement preparation, and multi-state tax returns — all tailored to your firm's structure.

Compliance

Outsourced CCO services, email monitoring, compliance program development, regulatory inspection support, and access to independent legal counsel.

Business Administration

Fully outsourced and licensed CFO/FINOP services, client billing, HR, payroll processing, benefits management, and employee onboarding.

Valuation & Succession

Business valuations, succession roadmaps, exit strategy blueprints, and strategic consulting to help you know your worth while making key decisions.

Operations

Middle and back-office support including paperwork processing, daily reconciliation, custodian administration, document imaging, and custom reporting.

Process Improvement

Workflow analysis and optimization designed to streamline your operations, reduce friction, and free your team to focus on higher-value activities.

Orion Consulting

Expert guidance on the Orion platform — implementation, customization, and ongoing support to get the most from your portfolio management technology.

Transitions

Customized advisor transition plans, custodian relationship facilitation, asset reviews, client communications, and account onboarding support.
